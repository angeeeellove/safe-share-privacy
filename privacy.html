Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SafeShare ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and payment information (for Pro subscribers)
  • User Content: The content you encrypt and share via SafeShare cards
  • Preferences: Language, theme choices, and app settings

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Features used, cards created, app performance metrics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (for security purposes only)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the SafeShare service
  • Processing transactions and sending related information
  • Sending technical notices and support messages
  • Responding to comments, questions, and customer service requests
  • Monitoring and analyzing trends, usage, and activities
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues and fraudulent activity

4. Data Storage and Security

Zero-Knowledge Architecture: SafeShare uses end-to-end encryption. Your card content is encrypted locally on your device BEFORE being uploaded to our servers. We cannot access, read, or decrypt your shared content.

Additional security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Cards are automatically deleted after being viewed
  • We retain minimal metadata necessary for service operation
  • Payment information is processed by third-party providers (Apple/Google) and is never stored on our servers

5. Data Retention

  • Active Cards: Retained until viewed by recipient (auto-deleted)
  • Expired Cards: Deleted 24 hours after expiration
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Logs: Anonymized and deleted after 90 days

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or asset transfer
  • Protection: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@safeshare.app

8. Children's Privacy

SafeShare is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

  • Email: privacy@safeshare.app
  • Website: https://safeshare.app

隐私政策

最后更新：2026年1月

1. 引言

欢迎使用 SafeShare（"我们"、"我们的"）。我们致力于保护您的个人信息和您的隐私权。本隐私政策说明了当您使用我们的移动应用程序时，我们如何收集、使用、披露和保护您的信息。

2. 我们收集的信息

2.1 您提供的信息

  • 账户信息：当您创建账户时，我们收集您的电子邮件地址和支付信息（针对Pro订阅者）
  • 用户内容：您通过SafeShare卡片加密和分享的内容
  • 偏好设置：语言、主题选择和应用设置

2.2 自动收集的信息

  • 使用数据：使用的功能、创建的卡片、应用性能指标
  • 设备信息：设备类型、操作系统、唯一设备标识符
  • 位置数据：基于IP地址的近似位置（仅用于安全目的）

3. 我们如何使用您的信息

我们将收集的信息用于：

  • 提供和维护SafeShare服务
  • 处理交易并发送相关信息
  • 发送技术通知和支持消息
  • 回应评论、问题和客户服务请求
  • 监控和分析趋势、使用情况和活动
  • 检测、预防和处理技术问题和欺诈活动

4. 数据存储和安全

零知识架构：SafeShare使用端到端加密。您的卡片内容在上传到我们的服务器之前会在您的设备上本地加密。我们无法访问、读取或解密您分享的内容。

额外的安全措施：

  • 所有数据在传输过程中均使用TLS 1.3加密
  • 卡片在被查看者查看后自动删除
  • 我们仅保留服务运行所需的最少元数据
  • 支付信息由第三方提供商（Apple/Google）处理，绝不存储在我们的服务器上

5. 数据保留

  • 有效卡片：保留至被查看者查看为止（自动删除）
  • 过期卡片：在过期后24小时内删除
  • 账户数据：在您的账户处于活动状态期间保留
  • 使用日志：匿名化并在90天后删除

6. 数据共享和披露

我们不会出售您的个人数据。我们仅在以下情况下共享您的信息：

  • 服务提供商：与协助我们运营服务的可信第三方
  • 法律要求：当法律、法院命令或政府法规要求时
  • 业务转让：在合并、出售或资产转让过程中
  • 保护：保护我们的权利、隐私、安全或财产

7. 您的隐私权利

根据您所在的地区，您可能拥有以下权利：

  • 访问：请求获取您的个人数据副本
  • 更正：请求更正不准确的数据
  • 删除：请求删除您的个人数据
  • 反对：反对处理您的个人数据
  • 限制：请求限制处理您的个人数据
  • 数据可携带：请求将您的数据传输到另一项服务

要行使这些权利，请联系我们：privacy@safeshare.app

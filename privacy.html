Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

SafeShare Core Principle: No registration required. No email needed. No account to manage. Your data stays private.

1. Introduction

Welcome to SafeShare. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when you use our service. SafeShare is designed with privacy-first principles: minimal data collection, zero-knowledge encryption, and no tracking.

2. No Registration Required

Important: SafeShare does NOT require you to create an account, provide an email address, or register in any way. You can start using the app immediately without sharing any personal information.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Card Content: The content you encrypt and share via SafeShare cards (encrypted locally before upload)
  • App Preferences: Your chosen language and card theme (stored locally on your device only)

3.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Card Creation Count: Number of cards created (stored locally, used for free tier limit)

What We DON'T Collect:

  • No email addresses
  • No phone numbers
  • No user accounts or identities
  • No device identifiers or tracking
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking

4. How We Use Information

Information collected is used solely for:

  • Providing core SafeShare functionality (creating and sharing encrypted cards)
  • Enforcing free tier limits (cards created per month)
  • Remembering your app preferences (language, theme)

We do NOT use your information for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose beyond app functionality.

5. Data Storage and Security

Zero-Knowledge Architecture: Your card content is encrypted locally on your device using AES-256-GCM BEFORE being uploaded to our servers. The encryption key is never sent to our servers. We cannot access, read, or decrypt your shared content.

Additional security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Cards are automatically and permanently deleted after being viewed once
  • Expired cards are deleted after 24 hours
  • We retain only minimal metadata necessary for card delivery
  • No personal information is stored on our servers

6. Data Retention

  • Active Cards: Retained on server until viewed by recipient (auto-deleted)
  • Expired Cards: Deleted 24 hours after expiration time
  • Local Preferences: Stored on your device until you change them or uninstall the app
  • Card History: Stored locally on your device only

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data. We do not have personal data to share.

We may share non-personal metadata only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With hosting infrastructure providers (e.g., Cloudflare) solely for operating the service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation

8. Your Privacy Rights

Because SafeShare does not collect personal information and requires no registration:

  • No Account to Delete: Since there are no user accounts, there is nothing to delete from our servers
  • No Data to Export: Your data is already on your device (app preferences and card history)
  • Local Control: You can clear your local data at any time through the app settings

9. Children's Privacy

SafeShare is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents or guardians should supervise minors' use of the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

  • Email: privacy@safeshare.app
  • Website: https://safeshare.app

隐私政策

最后更新：2026年1月

SafeShare 核心原则：无需注册。无需邮箱。无需管理账户。您的数据保持私密。

1. 引言

欢迎使用 SafeShare。本隐私政策说明了应用在使用服务时如何处理信息。SafeShare 采用隐私优先的设计原则：最少数据收集、零知识加密、无追踪。

2. 无需注册

重要提示：SafeShare 不要求您创建账户、提供电子邮件地址或以任何方式注册。您可以立即开始使用应用，无需分享任何个人信息。

3. 我们收集的信息

3.1 您提供的信息

  • 卡片内容：您通过 SafeShare 卡片加密和分享的内容（在上传前本地加密）
  • 应用偏好：您选择的语言和卡片主题（仅存储在您的设备上）

3.2 自动收集的信息

  • 卡片创建计数：创建的卡片数量（本地存储，用于免费版限制）

我们不收集：

  • 不收集电子邮件地址
  • 不收集电话号码
  • 不收集用户账户或身份信息
  • 不收集设备标识符或追踪信息
  • 不收集位置数据
  • 不收集使用分析或行为追踪

4. 我们如何使用信息

收集的信息仅用于：

  • 提供 SafeShare 核心功能（创建和分享加密卡片）
  • 执行免费版限制（每月创建的卡片数）
  • 记住您的应用偏好（语言、主题）

我们不会将您的信息用于广告、分析或应用功能之外的任何其他目的。

5. 数据存储和安全

零知识架构：您的卡片内容在上传到我们的服务器之前，使用 AES-256-GCM 在您的设备上本地加密。加密密钥从未发送到我们的服务器。我们无法访问、读取或解密您分享的内容。

额外的安全措施：

  • 所有数据在传输过程中均使用 HTTPS/TLS 加密
  • 卡片在被查看者查看一次后自动永久删除
  • 过期卡片在过期时间后 24 小时内删除
  • 我们仅保留卡片传递所需的最少元数据
  • 我们的服务器上不存储个人信息

6. 数据保留

  • 有效卡片：保留在服务器上直到被查看者查看（自动删除）
  • 过期卡片：在过期时间后 24 小时内删除
  • 本地偏好：存储在您的设备上，直到您更改或卸载应用
  • 卡片历史：仅存储在您的设备上

7. 数据共享和披露

我们不会出售、出租或共享您的个人数据。我们没有可共享的个人数据。

我们仅在以下情况下共享非个人元数据：

  • 服务提供商：与托管基础设施提供商（如 Cloudflare）仅用于运营服务
  • 法律要求：当法律、法院命令或政府法规要求时

8. 您的隐私权利

由于 SafeShare 不收集个人信息且无需注册：

  • 无需删除账户：由于没有用户账户，无需从我们的服务器删除任何内容
  • 无需导出数据：您的数据已在您的设备上（应用偏好和卡片历史）
  • 本地控制：您可以随时通过应用设置清除本地数据

9. 儿童隐私

SafeShare 不适用于 13 岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。父母或监护人应监督未成年人使用本应用。

10. 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何更改将在此页面上发布，并更新修订日期。更改后继续使用应用即表示接受更新后的政策。

如果您对本隐私政策有疑问，请联系：
